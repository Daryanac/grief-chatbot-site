Trust & safety

Clear boundaries. Transparent consent. Supportive technology—never a replacement for people.

Important

  • Lantern is a voice chatbot for grief support. It is not therapy or medical care.
  • Voice features are opt-in. A loved one’s voice model is only used with clear consent and disclosures.
  • Lantern does not claim your loved one is alive. It is a supportive simulation with boundaries.
  • You control consent. Privacy/consent are shown on the landing page and again during onboarding.
  • If you are in crisis, use the crisis resources below and seek immediate help.
See crisis resources Read FAQ

The voice feature is optional and requires explicit consent. Recordings you provide would be used to create a voice model for spoken responses, with clear disclosure that it is a simulation and not the real person.

This landing page is a prototype. In the final product, we would clearly list what data is collected, why it is needed, and what you can delete or opt out of.

Lantern reinforces boundaries (for example, “I am a tool, not a person”), encourages breaks, and promotes reaching out to trusted humans and campus support.

You will see a plain-language consent and privacy summary on the landing page, and again during onboarding before any personalization features.

Crisis support

If you feel like you might hurt yourself or someone else, contact local emergency services now. In the United States, you can call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
