Background and Significance

Grief can affect mental and physical health. Our concept focuses on accessible support for college students.

Existing Solutions and Gap

  • Existing tools provide pieces of the solution.
  • Gap: grief-specific voice support with clear safeguards.

Proposed Solution

  • Voice conversation flow with structured coping prompts.
  • User-controlled memory settings.
  • Advice Memory Mode with consent and disclosure.
