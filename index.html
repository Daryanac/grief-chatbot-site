A gentle way to continue the bond

Grief does not always need more noise. Sometimes it needs a quieter place to remember, reflect, and hear something familiar. Lantern is designed to offer a steady, respectful experience that feels warm without feeling overwhelming.

Gentler starting point

Voice + conversation

For users who want the comfort of hearing and talking with their loved one without adding a visual likeness. This can feel quieter, more private, and easier to return to.

  • Focus stays on voice and memory
  • Lower emotional intensity
  • Simple, familiar, and private
Optional only

Voice + gentle visual presence

For users who feel more connected with a soft visual layer. This would stay optional, clearly explained, and always under the user’s control.

  • Never forced
  • More immersive if desired
  • Boundaries remain explicit

Trust & safety

Designed for connection, but grounded in consent, boundaries, and support when emotions feel too heavy.

Important

  • Lantern supports continuing bonds. It offers a voice-based space for reflection, comfort, and connection after loss.
  • Lantern is AI-generated. It is shaped by recordings, memories, and user input. It is not literally bringing someone back.
  • Consent comes first. Users should understand what is uploaded, what is generated, and what they can edit or remove.
  • Emotional pacing matters. Reminders, boundaries, and check-ins help keep the experience grounded.
  • Real support stays visible. If the experience becomes overwhelming, Lantern should encourage breaks and point users toward human help.
