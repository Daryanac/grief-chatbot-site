Common questions

Short answers up front, with details if you want them.

Is this a replacement for therapy or counseling?
No. Lantern is supportive technology, not therapy and not a substitute for professional care or human support.
How is my privacy protected?
We design for data minimization and clear consent. The site will explain what information is collected, why, and how you can delete or opt out.
Does it really ‚Äúunderstand‚Äù grief?
It cannot fully understand grief like a person can. It can offer reflective prompts and resources, and it should never pretend to be a human or your loved one.
What if I am in crisis?
If you are in immediate danger, call 911 (US) or your local emergency number. In the US, you can call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
Will this use voice replication?
Because many people reported discomfort with voice replication, any voice features would be strictly opt-in, with explicit consent and clear boundaries.
How much does it cost?
This is a class project prototype. If we ever expand it, pricing (if any) would be disclosed clearly and never hidden behind dark patterns.

Privacy & Consent (summary)

Consent first: We will only enable sensitive features (like voice replication) with explicit, informed opt-in.
Data minimization: Collect the minimum needed, explain why, and provide a clear deletion path.
Boundaries: The companion must not claim to be a loved one or encourage dependence.
Crisis: If a user indicates self-harm risk, the site routes to real help (988/911).
