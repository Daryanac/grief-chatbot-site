Safety and privacy are the product

Your feedback emphasized that anything ‚Äúsketchy‚Äù or unclear would make you leave quickly ‚Äî so we keep this visible at all times.

Consent-first design

  • Consent is required before any sensitive feature can be used.
  • Users can opt out and still access non-voice coping support.
  • We repeat consent and disclosure on landing + onboarding.

Dependency + boundaries

  • We do not claim to be your loved one or replace them.
  • We discourage reliance and encourage human support.
  • We include crisis routing and resource prompts when needed.
If you are in crisis
